Jesse's Favorite Video Games

My top 3 video games of all time!

Legends of Runeterra

This card game is my favorite because the game is beuatifully designed. The game is always balanced and allows for player-interaction unseen in other card games.

Brawl Stars

This game is just fun because it's simple. It's just a bunch of characters each with their own attacks and abilities brawling it out in various game modes.

Pac-Man 256

It's classic pac-man with a twist! You can unlock unique abilities to destroy ghosts! Or like in one of my favorite abilities, sneak past them!
